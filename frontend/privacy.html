Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 February 2026

1. Overview

Seaside Beacon ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to keeping things minimal — we only collect what is necessary to deliver the Service.

2. Data We Collect

Data Purpose Stored Where
Email address Account creation, daily forecast emails, subscription management MongoDB (encrypted at rest)
Name (optional) Personalisation of emails and greetings MongoDB
Password (hashed) Account authentication MongoDB (bcrypt hash, never stored in plain text)
Beach preference Sending you the forecast for the beach you care about MongoDB
Telegram chat ID Linking your Telegram account for alerts and the AI assistant MongoDB
Payment info Processing Premium subscriptions Razorpay (we never store card details — Razorpay handles this)
Chat messages AI assistant conversation context (per-session, in-memory only) Server RAM (not persisted — cleared on restart or after 2 hours of inactivity)
Community feedback Sunrise accuracy ratings and optional comments MongoDB

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not track your location or use GPS data.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not store your credit card or bank details — all payment processing is handled by Razorpay.
  • We do not use analytics trackers or embed third-party tracking scripts.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Email delivery: Sending your daily sunrise forecasts (8:30 PM preview and 4 AM morning alert) and account-related notifications.
  • Forecast personalisation: Showing you the forecast for your selected beach.
  • Telegram alerts: Sending sunrise score notifications and AI assistant responses to your linked Telegram account.
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymised community feedback helps us calibrate the scoring algorithm.
  • Subscription management: Processing payments and managing your Premium access status via Razorpay.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Open-Meteo — Weather forecast data. No user data is sent; we only query public weather APIs.
  • Razorpay — Payment processing. Your payment data is handled directly by Razorpay under their security standards (PCI DSS compliant). We receive only subscription status, not card details.
  • Telegram — Messaging platform for alerts and the AI assistant. Your Telegram chat ID is stored to deliver messages. Message content is processed in-memory and not persisted.
  • Google (OAuth) — If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email. We do not access your Google contacts, files, or any other data.
  • AI Providers (Gemini, Groq) — Chat messages are sent to AI providers to generate responses. These providers process messages per their terms and do not retain conversation data beyond request processing.
  • Resend — Email delivery service. Your email address is shared with Resend solely for the purpose of delivering forecast emails.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored or transmitted in plain text.
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens transmitted over HTTPS only.
  • Database access is restricted to the application server with authentication credentials.
  • The Service is served entirely over HTTPS (TLS encrypted in transit).
  • AI assistant conversations are stored in server memory only and automatically purged after 2 hours of inactivity or on server restart.

No system is 100% secure. While we take care to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Free subscribers: Email and beach preference are retained until you unsubscribe. Unsubscribing removes your data from the mailing list.
  • Premium accounts: Account data is retained until you cancel your subscription and request deletion.
  • Chat history: Not persisted. Cleared after 2 hours of inactivity or when the server restarts.
  • Community feedback: Retained indefinitely to support algorithm calibration. Feedback does not contain personally identifying information beyond what you voluntarily submit.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and associated data. Email us and we will process the request within 7 days.
  • Unsubscribe — Every forecast email contains an unsubscribe link. One click, no questions.
  • Data portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format.

9. Children's Privacy

Seaside Beacon is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify Premium subscribers via email.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, reach out at support@seasidebeacon.com.
