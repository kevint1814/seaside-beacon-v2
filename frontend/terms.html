What Seaside Beacon does

We're India's first sunrise quality forecast built specifically for Chennai's beaches — Marina, Elliot's (Besant Nagar), Covelong, and Thiruvanmiyur. We analyse atmospheric conditions and give each morning a score from 0 to 100, so you can decide if it's worth setting that 5 AM alarm.

The service includes sunrise scoring, a 7-day forecast calendar, photography guidance, email alerts, Telegram notifications, an AI sunrise assistant with built-in customer support, and a community feedback system.

About our forecasts

Here's the honest truth: our scores are predictions, not promises. They're based on atmospheric models, satellite data, and a scoring algorithm we've carefully built — but weather has a mind of its own. A score of 75 means conditions look great, not that the sunrise is guaranteed to be spectacular.

We do our best to be accurate, but we can't be held responsible if a forecast doesn't quite match reality. That's nature for you. If a score felt off, we'd love your feedback on the website — it genuinely helps us improve.

Free vs Premium

Free users get a daily sunrise forecast email at 4 AM IST with the score, basic conditions, and an honest recommendation on whether to go. No sign-up pressure, no expiry — it's free forever.

Premium users get everything free users get, plus an evening preview email at 8:30 PM to plan ahead, DSLR and mobile camera settings tailored to conditions, AI photography insights, the 7-day forecast calendar, beach comparison, the AI sunrise assistant on Telegram (with photography guidance and customer support), and priority alerts for 70+ score mornings.

Premium subscription

Premium is ₹49/month or ₹399/year, billed through Razorpay. Your subscription renews automatically — but you can cancel anytime from your account settings. When you cancel, you'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

We don't do partial refunds for unused time, but if something goes wrong on our end — like a technical issue that blocks your access for a while — reach out and we'll sort it out fairly. We may adjust pricing in the future, but we'll always give existing subscribers at least 30 days' notice.
